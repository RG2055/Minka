Install on iPhone
Safari on iPhone installs web apps through the Share menu. If the automatic dialog does not appear, use these steps:
1
Open the Share menuTap the Share button in Safari’s bottom toolbar.
2
Choose “Add to Home Screen”Scroll the action list if you do not see it right away.
3
Tap “Add”The app will then launch as a standalone home screen app.
